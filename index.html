Selling your Home?Let us Stage it

Home staging is about showing a property in its best possible light, it helps potential buyers imagine themselves in the space. Many buyers like to see what a room really looks like “lived in”, some have a hard time envisioning themselves living in a vacant home. Vacant homes can feel cold and abandoned and appear smaller than they really are, putting furniture in a room gives buyers a much better frame of reference.

Contact Us Today!

Vacant homes that are well staged net approximately $4,000 more on their sale.

Vacant and non-staged homes lost on average $10,000, that is a $14,000 difference!

If they had spent $3,000 on staging, they would have made over $11,000 more on the sale of their home*

*Average statistics taken from the Northwest Multiple Listing Service from 11/4/2018 to 1/3/2019, 98012 zip code

First impressions are key to attracting a buyer to your home.

Most buyers decide which homes to go see on the internet first, we stage homes with photography AND private showings in mind.

The design of the staging we provide is very individual to your home. It will compliment the features of your home, and what makes your home special and unique, plus create a "natural flow" for the way your house lives. This will maximize your chances of getting the most money for your home in the least amount of time. 

If you still reside in the home, we can use your furnishings, and add some of our own. We tailor our services and options to your specific needs and budget.
